Dinners

An unforgettable evening

Whether it is the first night before the initiation of the conference so you want everyone to mingle and get to know each other better, or the last night after too many meetings and before a flight or two away home one can organise an unforgettable Dinner at Popeye Village – the only Film Set in Europe.
The Head Chef
and the rest of the team can prepare ludicrously tasty meals for all the group to enjoy. We have a number of menus varying from finger food items for stand up events, large Barbeque menus compromising of meat, poultry and fish dishes accompanied by an array of freshly prepared salads, home-made pasta dishes and other sides, a NEW stand-up Barbeque menu as well as our popular Western Themed menu.
Together with these menus we have a number of beverage packages as well as open-bar packages.
Contact us now on: info@popeyemalta.com / 00356 21 52 47 82
The whole group
can arrive either by coach or by boats and be greeted in our main square by Popeye and the gang and served welcome drinks whilst the visitors can freely roam around the Film Set and enjoy the stunningly beautiful sun-set scene facing Anchor Bay.
The event can be held on one of the terraces or the Rough House Hall overlooking Anchor Bay and surrounded by Popeye’s Film Set on one side and the beautiful Maltese Cliffs on the other.
The film Set can be taken on Exclusivity basis too and an after-party can also follow the nice meal whilst dancing to favourite tunes played by a local DJ or band.
