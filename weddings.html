Have the Fairytale Wedding

You have been dreaming about a location so unique you and your guests will remember it forever! Only at Popeye Village, where we make your dream day a reality.

We can understand that it is not as easy to find the most suitable location for your wedding, especially if you are looking for a place that offers: originality, character, flair and something different from the norm. So, we are offering you a set, a complete village as the back drop for the wedding they have always been dreaming of.
The 33 year old film set
Has a personality of its own. Its shabby wooden houses together with the stunning, sun-set facing Anchor Bay will allow your clients and all of their guests to enjoy their day to the maximum at their desired location. The film set offers a number of spots around the village according to the number of guests. Whether they choose the heart or the village or a beach wedding, Popeye Village will surely be the best choice for them.
The set is available for local and foreign weddings between Monday to Sunday to best fit with your clients’ desires and always according to availability with the event being held after opening hours.
